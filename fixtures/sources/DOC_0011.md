# Investment Policy Statement

**Prepared for:** Catherine O'Brien
**Date:** 2026-03-31
**Review Frequency:** Annual

## Investment Objective
The primary investment objective is capital preservation. The client
seeks to achieve long-term capital appreciation while managing downside risk consistent with
their stated tolerance level.

## Risk Profile
- **Risk Tolerance:** Moderate
- **Time Horizon:** 20 years
- **Maximum Acceptable Drawdown:** 25%

## Target Asset Allocation
| Asset Class    | Target (%) | Range (%)     |
|---------------|-----------|---------------|
| Equities      | 77.6      | 67.6-87.6 |
| Fixed Income  | 9.4      | 0-19.4 |
| Cash & Equiv. | 13.0       | 0-23.0         |

## Constraints
- No investments in tobacco or firearms manufacturers
- Maximum single-issuer concentration: 5% of portfolio value
- All fixed income must be investment grade (BBB- or above)


## Special Provisions and Bespoke Constraints

  - Subject to quarterly review, the portfolio will maintain a currency hedge ratio of at least 50% on non-USD denominated holdings.
  - The portfolio must maintain a minimum 15% allocation to ESG-screened securities as per the client's environmental mandate dated 2026-01-15.
  - Notwithstanding the general allocation guidelines, the client has requested a specific carve-out of up to 5% in direct private equity co-investments.

## Rebalancing
The portfolio shall be rebalanced quarterly or when any asset class deviates more than
5 percentage points from its target allocation.

## Benchmark
Performance will be measured against the MSCI ACWI.

## Tax Considerations
The client is in the highest marginal tax bracket. Tax-loss harvesting should be
pursued opportunistically. Municipal bonds are preferred for fixed income allocation.
